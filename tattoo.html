In 2013, ownwer Nick and Sarah opened Great Lakes Tattoo in Chicago's west loop neighbourhood. This shop is one of the kind space with an attitude free atmosphere that celebrates and honors the traditions of tattooing: past, present and future. Considered one of the best tattoo shop in Chicago. Great Lakes Tattoo also houses the great state gallery an art and event space that hosts art shows, story telling nights, live music and more.

Great Lakes Tattoo is a destination shop for Chicago residents and tourists alike. The tattooers at GLT do designs of all kinds, including traditional American, Japanese, portraits, black and grey, tribal and more. Tattooers are available for appointments and same-day appointments everyday. Check out some of the best tattoos being done in Chicago on the portfolio page
